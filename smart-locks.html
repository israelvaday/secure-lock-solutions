Smart Lock Installation

Upgrade your home or business to keyless convenience with professional smart lock installation from Secure Lock Solutions. Control your locks from anywhere with your smartphone.

Why Smart Locks?

  • Keyless Convenience: No more fumbling for keys or worrying about lost keys
  • Remote Access: Lock and unlock from anywhere via smartphone
  • Guest Access: Create temporary codes for visitors, cleaners, dog walkers
  • Activity Logs: See who came and went and when
  • Auto-Lock: Doors lock automatically after you leave
  • Smart Home Integration: Works with Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit

Types of Smart Locks

WiFi Smart Locks

Connect directly to your home WiFi for remote access from anywhere. No hub required. Popular models include Schlage Encode and August WiFi Smart Lock.

Bluetooth Smart Locks

Unlock automatically when you approach with your phone. Great for quick, hands-free entry. Often combined with WiFi bridge for remote access.

Keypad Smart Locks

Enter a PIN code instead of using a key. Perfect for families, rentals, and offices. Create multiple codes and revoke them anytime.

Biometric Smart Locks

Fingerprint recognition for ultimate security and convenience. Your finger is your key—it can't be lost or copied.

Our Installation Process

  1. Assess your door and existing hardware
  2. Recommend the best smart lock for your needs
  3. Professional installation with proper alignment
  4. Connect to your WiFi and smartphone
  5. Program user codes and settings
  6. Demonstrate all features and answer questions

Smart Lock for Rentals & Airbnb

Smart locks are perfect for vacation rentals and Airbnb properties. Create unique codes for each guest, automatically expire them at checkout, and never worry about lost keys or changing locks between guests.
