Commercial Locksmith Services

Comprehensive business security solutions — from master key systems to advanced access control for offices, retail stores, and industrial facilities throughout Orange County.

Commercial Lockout Services

Secure Lock Solutions understands that being locked out of your business costs time and money. We provide 24/7 emergency commercial lockout services. Whether it's your office, store, or warehouse, our rapid-response team gets you back to business fast without damaging your property.

Master Key Systems

Simplify access management with professional master key systems. We design and implement key hierarchies that allow managers to access all areas while restricting employee access to specific zones. Perfect for offices, hotels, apartment complexes, and retail chains.

Access Control Systems

Upgrade your business security with modern access control systems. Our experts install keycard systems, keypad entry, biometric scanners, and smartphone-enabled access. Track who enters your building and when, with detailed access logs and remote management.

High-Security Commercial Locks

Protect your business assets with commercial-grade high-security locks. Secure Lock Solutions installs Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, and ASSA ABLOY systems that resist picking, bumping, and drilling. We also provide restricted keyway systems to prevent unauthorized key duplication.

Panic Bars & Exit Devices

Ensure safe evacuation routes with properly installed panic hardware. We install and service panic bars, exit devices, and crash bars that comply with fire codes and ADA requirements. Keep your employees safe while maintaining security against unauthorized entry.

File Cabinet & Safe Locks

Secure sensitive documents and valuables with professional file cabinet and safe lock services. We open, repair, and change combinations on office safes and filing cabinets. Protect your confidential business information with upgraded security.

Lock Maintenance Contracts

Keep your business locks in top condition with scheduled maintenance. We offer service contracts for regular inspection, lubrication, and adjustment of all locks and access control systems. Prevent lockouts and security issues before they happen.
