Residential Locksmith Services

Secure Lock Solutions provides comprehensive residential locksmith services throughout Orange County, CA. From emergency lockouts to complete home security upgrades, our expert technicians ensure your home is safe and secure.

Home Lockout Services

Locked out of your house? Don't panic! Our emergency lockout specialists are available 24/7 to get you back inside quickly and safely. We use non-destructive entry techniques to open your door without damage, getting you back into your home fast.

Lock Change & Replacement

Whether you've moved into a new home, lost your keys, or want to upgrade your security, we provide professional lock change services. We install high-quality deadbolts, knob locks, and lever handles from trusted brands like Schlage, Kwikset, and Medeco.

Lock Rekeying

Rekeying is a cost-effective way to change your locks without replacing the entire hardware. Perfect for new homeowners, after a break-in, or when keys are lost. We can rekey all your locks to work with a single key for convenience.

Smart Lock Installation

Upgrade to keyless convenience with smart lock installation. We install and program WiFi-enabled locks, Bluetooth locks, and keypad entry systems from brands like August, Yale, and Schlage Encode. Control your locks from anywhere with your smartphone.

High-Security Lock Systems

Protect your family and valuables with high-security lock systems. We install bump-proof, pick-resistant, and drill-resistant locks that provide maximum protection against break-ins. Our experts will assess your home and recommend the best security solutions.

Garage & Gate Locks

Secure all entry points to your home including garages and gates. We install and repair garage door locks, gate locks, and padlocks. Don't overlook these important security points—let us help you protect your entire property.
