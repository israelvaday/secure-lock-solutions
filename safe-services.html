Safe Services

Secure Lock Solutions provides complete safe services throughout Orange County. From emergency safe opening to new safe installation, our certified technicians handle all types of safes.

Safe Opening

Locked out of your safe? We use professional techniques to open safes without damage when possible. Common situations we handle:

  • Forgotten combinations
  • Inherited safes with unknown combinations
  • Electronic lock battery failure
  • Malfunctioning locks
  • Jammed bolts

Combination Changes

Changed the combination for security? We change combinations on mechanical and electronic safes. Recommended after:

  • Employee termination
  • Suspected compromise
  • Buying a used safe
  • Routine security maintenance

Types of Safes We Service

Home Safes

Fire safes, document safes, jewelry safes, and wall safes. Protect your valuables, important documents, and irreplaceable items from fire and theft.

Gun Safes

California law requires firearms to be stored securely. We install and service gun safes of all sizes, from handgun boxes to full walk-in vaults. Biometric, electronic, and mechanical locks available.

Commercial Safes

Cash safes, deposit safes, drop safes, and high-security vaults for businesses. TL-15, TL-30, and TRTL-rated safes for maximum security.

Floor Safes

Hidden in concrete floors for ultimate concealment. Installation and service of in-floor safes.

New Safe Sales & Installation

We help you select the right safe for your needs and budget. Considerations include:

  • Fire rating (30 min, 1 hr, 2 hr)
  • Burglary rating (RSC, B, C, TL-15, TL-30)
  • Size and capacity
  • Lock type (electronic, mechanical, biometric)
  • Anchoring requirements

Professional installation includes delivery, positioning, anchoring to floor or wall, and programming.

Safe Moving

Safes are heavy—sometimes thousands of pounds. We have the equipment and expertise to move safes safely without damaging your floors, walls, or the safe itself.
