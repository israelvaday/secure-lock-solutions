What Counts as an Emergency?

  • Locked out of your home, car, or business
  • Break-in and need locks changed immediately
  • Key broke off in the lock
  • Lock is jammed and you can't secure your property
  • Lost keys and worried about security
  • Child or pet locked in car

If you're not sure if your situation is an emergency, call us anyway. We're here to help.
