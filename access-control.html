Access Control Systems

Secure Lock Solutions designs and installs commercial access control systems throughout Orange County. Control who enters your building with keycard, keypad, biometric, or smartphone-based systems.

Keycard & Fob Systems

The most common commercial access control solution. Employees use proximity cards or key fobs to unlock doors. Easy to issue new credentials and revoke access when employees leave. Ideal for offices, warehouses, and multi-tenant buildings.

Keypad Entry Systems

Enter a PIN code to unlock. No cards to carry or lose. Create individual codes for each user to track access. Perfect for smaller businesses, back doors, and restricted areas within a building.

Biometric Access Control

Fingerprint, facial recognition, or iris scanning for the highest level of security. Impossible to share or duplicate credentials. Ideal for data centers, laboratories, and high-security areas.

Smartphone-Based Access

Modern systems allow employees to use their smartphones as credentials. Bluetooth or NFC-based unlocking, mobile credential management, and cloud-based administration.

Features & Capabilities

  • Time-Based Access: Set hours when each employee can enter
  • Zone Control: Restrict access to specific areas by user
  • Audit Trails: Complete logs of all entry attempts
  • Remote Management: Add/remove users from anywhere
  • Integration: Connect with cameras, alarms, and building systems
  • Emergency Lockdown: Secure all doors instantly if needed
