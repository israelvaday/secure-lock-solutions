Lock Change & Rekey Services

Secure Lock Solutions provides professional lock change and rekeying services throughout Orange County. Whether you need new locks or just want to change who has access, we have the right solution for you.

Lock Change / Lock Replacement

A lock change involves removing your existing lock and installing a completely new one. This is recommended when:

  • Your current locks are old, worn, or damaged
  • You want to upgrade to higher-security locks
  • You're switching to smart locks or keyless entry
  • Your locks don't meet current security standards
  • You want a different style or finish

We install quality locks from trusted brands including Schlage, Kwikset, Medeco, Baldwin, and more. Our locksmith can recommend the best lock for your security needs and budget.

Lock Rekeying

Rekeying changes the internal pins of your lock so old keys no longer work, and new keys are created. The lock hardware stays the same. Rekeying is ideal when:

  • You've moved into a new home
  • You've lost your keys
  • A roommate or employee has left
  • You want all locks to use one key
  • Your locks are in good condition but keys are compromised

Rekeying is typically less expensive than replacing locks and can be done quickly on-site.

When to Choose Each Option

Choose Lock Change When: Choose Rekeying When:
Locks are damaged or worn Locks work fine but keys are compromised
Want better security Moving into a new home
Upgrading to smart locks Lost keys but lock is fine
Changing style/appearance Budget is a concern
