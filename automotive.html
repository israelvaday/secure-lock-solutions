Automotive Locksmith Services

Full-service automotive locksmith solutions — from emergency car lockouts to advanced key programming for all vehicle makes and models throughout Orange County.

Car Lockout Service

Locked your keys in the car? Secure Lock Solutions provides fast, damage-free car lockout service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Our technicians use professional tools to safely unlock your vehicle without scratching the paint or damaging the door. We handle all makes and models.

Car Key Replacement

Lost all your car keys? No problem! We can create new keys for your vehicle even without the original. Our mobile units are equipped with key cutting machines and programming equipment to make replacement keys on-site. Much faster and often cheaper than the dealership.

Transponder Key Programming

Modern vehicles use transponder chips in the keys for anti-theft protection. We program transponder keys for most car brands including Toyota, Honda, Ford, Chevrolet, BMW, Mercedes, and more. Get properly programmed keys that start your car reliably.

Key Fob & Remote Programming

Need a new key fob or remote? We supply and program replacement key fobs and remotes for keyless entry and push-button start systems. Our programming covers remote lock/unlock, trunk release, and panic buttons for most vehicle brands.

Ignition Repair & Replacement

Having trouble with your ignition? Key won't turn or gets stuck? We diagnose and repair ignition problems, including worn cylinders, broken wafers, and damaged keys. When repair isn't possible, we provide complete ignition replacement with new matched keys.

Broken Key Extraction

Key broken off in the door or ignition? Don't try to remove it yourself — you could push it in further. Our locksmiths have specialized extraction tools to safely remove broken key pieces without damaging your lock or ignition cylinder.

Motorcycle & RV Keys

We don't just service cars! Secure Lock Solutions also provides locksmith services for motorcycles, RVs, boats, and ATVs. Lost your motorcycle key? Locked out of your RV? We can help with key replacement and lockout services for recreational vehicles too.
