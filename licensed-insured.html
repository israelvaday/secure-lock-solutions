Licensed & Insured Locksmith Services

Secure Lock Solutions is a fully licensed, insured, and bonded locksmith company serving Orange County, California. Your security is our business—and we take both seriously.

California State Licensed

We hold all required California state licenses for locksmith services. Our company complies with California Business and Professions Code regulations for locksmiths. This means we've met strict requirements for training, background checks, and professional standards.

Fully Insured & Bonded

We carry comprehensive general liability insurance to protect you and your property. In the unlikely event of any damage during service, you're fully covered. Our bond provides additional financial protection and demonstrates our commitment to professional, ethical business practices.

Background-Checked Technicians

Every Secure Lock Solutions technician undergoes thorough background screening. We only hire trustworthy individuals with clean records. When our locksmith arrives at your door, you can feel confident about letting them into your home or business.

Professional Training & Certification

Our locksmiths receive ongoing training in the latest security technologies, lock systems, and automotive key programming. We stay current with industry advances to provide you with the best possible service.

Why Licensing Matters

  • Consumer Protection: Licensed locksmiths are held to professional standards
  • Accountability: We can be held responsible for our work
  • Quality Assurance: Licensing requires demonstrated competency
  • Legal Compliance: We operate within California law
  • Insurance Coverage: Protection for you if anything goes wrong

Beware of Unlicensed Locksmiths

Unfortunately, some individuals operate as locksmiths without proper licensing. They may quote low prices on the phone then charge much more upon arrival, or use damaging techniques that require expensive repairs. Always ask for license verification before allowing anyone to work on your locks.
