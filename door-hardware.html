Door Hardware Services

Secure Lock Solutions installs and repairs all types of door hardware throughout Orange County. From residential handle upgrades to commercial panic bar installation.

Door Handles & Knobs

Upgrade your door handles for improved function and aesthetics. We install:

  • Lever handles (ADA-compliant)
  • Door knobs
  • Entry handlesets
  • Privacy and passage functions
  • Commercial-grade handles

Door Hinges

Worn or damaged hinges cause doors to sag, stick, or not close properly. We:

  • Replace standard hinges
  • Install security hinges with non-removable pins
  • Upgrade to heavy-duty hinges for heavy doors
  • Install continuous (piano) hinges
  • Adjust and repair existing hinges

Door Closers

Door closers ensure doors close automatically and controlled. Required by fire code on many commercial doors. We install and adjust:

  • Surface-mounted closers
  • Concealed closers
  • Heavy-duty commercial closers
  • Delayed-action closers
  • Hold-open closers

Panic Bars & Exit Devices

Required on emergency exits in commercial buildings. Push bar opens the door for quick egress. We install:

  • Rim panic devices
  • Mortise panic devices
  • Vertical rod devices
  • Alarmed exit devices
  • Fire-rated exit devices

Additional Hardware

  • Kick Plates: Protect bottom of doors from scuffs and damage
  • Door Stops: Wall or floor mounted to prevent damage
  • Peepholes: See who's at your door before opening
  • Door Chains: Allow partial opening for security
  • Mail Slots: Installation and repair
  • Weatherstripping: Seal gaps for energy efficiency

ADA Compliance

The Americans with Disabilities Act has specific requirements for door hardware in commercial buildings. We ensure your hardware meets ADA requirements including lever handles, proper opening force, and automatic openers where required.
