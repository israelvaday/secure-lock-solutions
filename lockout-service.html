Identification Required

For your protection and ours, we require proof that you have a right to access the property. Please have ready:

  • Driver's license or ID
  • Vehicle registration (for car lockouts)
  • Lease or utility bill (for home lockouts)
  • Business card or authorization (for commercial)

What NOT to Do When Locked Out

  • Don't break a window - More expensive than locksmith
  • Don't try to pick it yourself - May damage lock
  • Don't call an unlicensed "locksmith" - Often scammers
  • Don't leave car running - Call us ASAP
